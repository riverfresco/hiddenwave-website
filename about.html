Strategic Systems for Creative Growth

A think tank operating across creative production, commercial distribution and brand partnerships.

Who we are

Hidden Wave operates as a dynamic think tank for brands, studios and founders who move between fashion, art and culture. We focus on the moments where identity, storytelling and market structure meet — where a single decision can shift how a brand is seen, distributed and remembered.

Based between Milan and Shanghai, we draw from both independent and institutional perspectives: global positioning with a local, on–the–ground sensitivity.

What we do

01 — Creative Production

We support image–making, editorial concepts and spatial experiences that extend a brand’s world: from campaign and showroom narratives to collaborations between designers, artists and publishers.

02 — Commercial & Distribution

We map and design wholesale structures, showroom strategies and regional roll–outs. The focus is on sustainable sell–in, long–term relationships and the right partners — not just more points of sale.

03 — Brand Partnerships

We connect brands, galleries, retailers and cultural institutions, building frameworks for collaborations that feel natural rather than opportunistic, and that are backed by clear commercial logic.

How we work

  • Start with listening: we audit positioning, language and existing structures before suggesting change.
  • Build systems, not moments: every production or activation lives inside a long–term strategy.
  • Keep teams light: we assemble ad–hoc constellations of collaborators around each project.
  • Stay close to the market: feedback from buyers, distributors and audiences continually informs our work.

Hidden Wave can be engaged on a project basis or as an ongoing strategic partner, supporting internal teams, founders and studios as they navigate new phases of growth.
